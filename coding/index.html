Quran and Tajweed class

In this Quran and Tajweed class, you'll learn how to recite the Quran with proper pronunciation, intonation, and rhythm. You'll also learn the rules of Tajweed, the science of the correct recitation of the Quran. Through this class, you'll deepen your spiritual connection with the Quran, enhance your understanding of its meanings, and improve your ability to recite it beautifully. Whether you're a beginner or an advanced learner, this class will help you perfect your recitation of the Quran

Egyption Dialect

In this Egyptian Arabic class, you'll learn the basics of the language, including pronunciation, grammar, and vocabulary. You'll also gain an understanding of the culture and customs of Egypt. Whether you're a beginner or have some knowledge of the language, this class will help you communicate with locals and enrich your travel experiences

Arabic class

In this Arabic class, you'll learn Modern Standard Arabic (MSA), the formal language used in news, literature, and business in the Arab world. You'll develop your listening, speaking, reading, and writing skills, and acquire a solid foundation in Arabic grammar and vocabulary. Whether you want to expand your career opportunities, travel to Arabic-speaking countries, or simply learn a new language, this class will help you achieve your goals
